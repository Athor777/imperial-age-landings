The Story

In 2012, two childhood friends started a band in Moscow.

By 2022, that band had played 200+ shows across every European country, streamed concerts to nearly 100,000 people during the global lockdown, and built something nobody thought possible — all from behind the Iron Curtain.

Then the war started.

The two of us, Jane and Aor, left everything behind — our country, our band, our life — and started over in the UK with a completely new lineup.

That era — as a Russian band — ended in 2023. The original band stayed in Russia. We became a UK band with new members — Colin, Beardy, Daniel and Manuele.

A new chapter is already unfolding. New songs written. A new album crowdfunding launching in May. Big stages this summer — including Bloodstock main stage.

This is our new era — as a UK band.

Which means the live albums from the previous decade have to be archived. Every physical CD and DVD is sold out. They will never be repressed again. Ever.

Note: our studio albums — Turn the Sun Off, Legacy of Atlantis, New World — are staying available. This bundle is only about the live albums and Warrior Race.

But we couldn't let that entire live decade just disappear. So we've made one final archive.
